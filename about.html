Hi, I’m Jenna — a software developer with a strong technical foundation, a passion for clean, intuitive design, and a genuine love for the outdoors.

I earned my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Arizona State University with a 4.0 GPA, along with a minor in Business. I’m drawn to roles in front-end and back-end development, web development, and technical business or finance positions where I can combine analytical thinking with creativity.

As a military spouse, remote work is essential for me, and I’m seeking opportunities that allow me to contribute meaningfully while supporting the demands of military life. I bring strong organization, adaptability, and leadership to every project I take on.

My goal is to apply my skills and creativity to work that makes a meaningful impact and encourages exploration, innovation, and connection with the outdoors.
